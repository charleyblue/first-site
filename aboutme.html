Introduction

I am currently an electrician who is in the process of a reinvention. I love both the hands-on aspect of being an electrician as well as the intellectual necessity of applied science, but it is time to give my body a break from the day-in-day-out wear and tear of climbing up and down ladders, crawling under houses and machinery, bending conduit, and pulling wires and cables. It is time to give my bones a rest!

I have always had a keen interest in the aspects of creating pleasing designs and my first design exposure began way back while working as a landscaper. I began my adulthood career creating and building beautiful landscapes at homes and commercial sites and found it very rewarding. Again, between the body and the economy, I said enough after 15 years of this worthy occupation and I found the study of applied physics required for competency in the electrical field a worthy alternative pursuit.

This leads me to this current juncture, another 15 years later, with the development and perfection of my new passion of web design and development. A little less physically demanding but none the less, exciting.

My Dream Job

Although I like working on independent projects, I also like being part of a team effort. I am an eager student as well as teacher. I love to share with an interested person the lessons and insights I have picked up in my work life. I enjoy researching things and think that the Internet is fantastic for its ease of information access.

I want to take this passion and apply it to solving communications problems with web design. I would love to be part of a team and tasked to solve all kinds of these problems associated with presenting clear typography and graphic design to convey a message.
